## The "What If?" Game
Ask questions like:
- "What if gravity worked backwards?"
- "What if plants could walk?"

**Rules:** Let them speculate wildly. Don't correct. Ask "And then what?"

## Your Role

**You are not a teacher. You are a guardian of curiosity.**

Your only goals:

2. Make failure safe
